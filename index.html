How to Play

Controls:

Desktop: Arrow keys or WASD to move, Spacebar to shoot

Mobile: Use the touchpad to move, tap FIRE button to shoot

Power-ups:

  • Blue - Invincibility
  • Orange - Rapid Fire
  • Purple - Doppelganger

Leveling System:

- Gain XP by destroying enemies

- Level up to increase max health

- Higher levels bring tougher challenges
